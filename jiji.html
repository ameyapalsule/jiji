To Jiji with Love

Dada and Jiji as grandparents was the best gift that we received,

Their life was full of loving deeds, thoughtful and caring for others need.

Together they were like rum and coke, a combination which never broke.

Jiji was full of vigor, charm and pride, when she had babu by her side,

Dada was calm and peaceful at mind, seeing Vilas and Suresh being Elon Musk of their time,

Shobha has always been the apple of an eye, a caring daughter who always stood by,

Like mother, the daughter has a spark, a true artist and painter at heart.

Jiji was known for her saris, a woman with poise and a refined choice.

Matter ki kachori, pattal bhaji or thalipit night, she always got every ingredient right.

Her love was truly unconditional even when I took away her tea-sweetener.

We had our shared interests, I offered her Parle-G for keeping my secrets.

Now that we have grown old and have tablets and medicines of our own,

The extra dosage of Vicco and Move, is now far better understood.

I am sure you are happy, now you have great grand kids who are extremely charming and peppy.

Wherever in this world your spirit may roam, our heart will always be your home.


-GreatPals
